About the song

"Birds of a Feather" by Billie Eilish and Finneas is a hauntingly beautiful song about intense, unconditional love and emotional dependency. The lyrics depict a relationship so deep that the narrator feels they are inseparable from their partner, even to the point of death. The recurring line "Birds of a feather, we should stick together" symbolizes the bond between the two, much like how birds of the same kind naturally stick together inseparable and perfectly matched.
